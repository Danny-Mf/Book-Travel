What is FeWo-direkt?

FeWo-direkt is your trusted partner for booking holiday homes and apartments. As part of Vrbo, a leading online marketplace for vacation rentals, FeWo-direkt offers over two million unique accommodations, including villas, bungalows, apartments, cabins, and more. Secure payments, 24/7 support, and a Book with Confidence Guarantee make booking effortless. With a user-friendly website and practical filters, you can easily find your dream vacation rental within your budget for a stress-free holiday experience.

Benefits of renting a holiday home or apartment

Families and groups especially appreciate the benefits of vacation rentals: more space, privacy, and amenities. Enjoy greater flexibility and freedom compared to traditional hotels, whether you're planning a city break or a relaxing getaway with your pet in scenic surroundings. Looking for a cozy mountain cabin with a washing machine and hot tub? A beachfront house or a large villa in the south with a pool and fully equipped kitchen? No problem – vacation rentals come in all sizes and with all the amenities you need for your next family vacation, adventure with friends, or romantic weekend.
